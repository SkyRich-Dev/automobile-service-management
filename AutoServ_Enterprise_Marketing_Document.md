## Platform Overview

### 1. Service Operations --- The Heart of Your Workshop

Manage every vehicle service job through a structured, traceable digital workflow:

**11-Stage Service Lifecycle:**
Appointment > Check-in > Digital Inspection > Job Card > Estimate > Customer Approval > Task Execution > Quality Check > Billing > Delivery > Completed

- **Kanban & List Views** --- Visualize active jobs across all stages at a glance
- **Digital Inspections** --- Capture findings with photos, videos, and detailed checklists
- **Smart Technician Assignment** --- Match jobs to technicians based on their skill profiles
- **Service Bay Management** --- Track bay availability and utilization in real-time
- **Quality Control Loops** --- QC failures automatically route jobs back for rework
- **Customer Approval Workflow** --- Estimates can be revised and re-sent before work begins
- **AI-Powered Summaries** --- Auto-generated job card summaries for faster diagnosis review

---

### 2. Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Convert leads, retain customers, and grow your business:

- **Lead Pipeline** --- Track prospects from first contact through conversion with stage-based management
- **Customer 360 Profile** --- A single view of every customer: vehicles, service history, invoices, contracts, communications, payments, and job cards
- **Credit Risk Assessment** --- Real-time credit utilization tracking with LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH risk indicators
- **Support Ticketing** --- Manage customer complaints with SLA tracking and escalation
- **Campaign Management** --- Run targeted marketing campaigns for service reminders, promotions, and seasonal offers
- **Customer Scoring** --- Rule-based scoring to identify your most valuable customers
- **Multi-Channel Communication** --- Reach customers via Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and in-app notifications
- **Follow-Up Task Management** --- Never miss a customer interaction with automated task reminders

---

### 3. Inventory & Supply Chain

Keep your parts room organized and your suppliers accountable:

- **Real-Time Stock Tracking** --- Monitor stock levels with automatic low-stock and out-of-stock alerts
- **Part Reservations** --- Reserve parts for specific job cards to prevent allocation conflicts
- **Goods Receipt Notes (GRN)** --- Record and verify incoming deliveries against purchase orders
- **Stock Transfers** --- Move inventory between branches with full tracking
- **Purchase Requisitions & Orders** --- Streamlined procurement from request to delivery
- **Stock Adjustments & Returns** --- Handle damaged goods, returns, and corrections with audit trails
- **Stock Ledger** --- Complete transaction history for every part
- **Supplier Invoices** --- Track supplier billing with GST/tax breakdown
- **Inventory Analytics** --- Valuation reports, fast-moving item analysis, and dead stock identification

**10-Tab Inventory Dashboard:** Parts | Reservations | GRNs | Transfers | Requisitions | Adjustments | Alerts | Stock Ledger | Supplier Invoices | Analytics

---

### 4. Supplier Management

Build stronger vendor relationships with data-driven insights:

- **Supplier Directory** --- Centralized database shared across all branches
- **Purchase Order Lifecycle** --- Draft > Pending Approval > Approved > Ordered > Partially Received > Received
- **Supplier Performance Tracking** --- Evaluate vendors on delivery time, quality, and pricing
- **Credit & Payment Terms** --- Manage credit limits and outstanding balances per supplier

---

### 5. Contracts & AMC Management

Maximize recurring revenue with structured service agreements:

- **Contract Types** --- Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), Corporate Fleet packages, and custom service plans
- **Coverage Rules** --- Define what each contract covers with granular service-level rules
- **Consumption Tracking** --- Monitor contract utilization in real-time
- **Auto-Detection** --- System automatically identifies active contracts during job card creation
- **Fleet Vehicle Management** --- Track multiple vehicles under corporate contracts
- **Approval Workflows** --- Multi-level approval chains for new contracts and renewals
- **Expiry Alerts** --- Proactive notifications for contracts nearing expiration
- **Dashboard Analytics** --- Active contracts, total value, expiring soon, and utilization metrics

---

### 6. Accounts & Finance

Complete financial management with enterprise-grade accounting:

- **Chart of Accounts** --- Full double-entry bookkeeping with Assets, Liabilities, Income, Expenses, and Equity categories
- **Enhanced Invoicing** --- Create, issue, approve, and track invoices with complete lifecycle management
- **Payment Processing** --- Record and confirm payments with status tracking
- **Expense Management** --- Submit > Approve > Mark Paid workflow with category-based tracking
- **Customer Receivables** --- Aging reports with overdue tracking
- **Credit Notes** --- Issue and manage credit adjustments
- **Tax Management** --- GST-compliant with configurable tax rates (CGST, SGST, IGST)
- **Financial Dashboard** --- Revenue, expenses, receivables, payables, and profit margin KPIs with period filtering

**7-Tab Finance Dashboard:** Dashboard | Invoices | Payments | Expenses | Receivables | Credit Notes | Chart of Accounts

- **Payroll Processing** --- Generate payroll records with incentive and deduction calculations
- **Performance Metrics** --- Productivity tracking, rework counts, and utilization percentages

Make data-driven decisions with powerful analytics:

### 9. Enterprise Administration

Complete control over your system configuration:

- **Immutable Audit Logs** --- Complete trail of every configuration change

## Multi-Branch Architecture

AutoServ Enterprise is built for scale:

- **Hierarchical Structure** --- Headquarters, regional offices, and individual branches
- **Branch-Independent Data** --- Each branch sees only its own data; leadership sees everything
- **Branch Switcher** --- Instantly switch context between branches from the sidebar
- **Unified Reporting** --- Aggregate analytics across all locations
- **Shared Resources** --- Suppliers are shared across branches; inventory and staff are branch-specific
- **Regional Management** --- Regional Managers oversee multiple branches with appropriate access

## Security & Compliance

- **Enterprise-Grade Authentication** --- Session-based authentication with CSRF protection
- **RBAC Enforcement** --- Every API endpoint validates role permissions before granting access
- **Default-Deny Policy** --- Users without proper role assignment are denied access by default
- **Immutable Audit Trail** --- Every service event, financial transaction, and configuration change is permanently logged
- **Input Validation** --- Protection against SQL injection, XSS, and other common attack vectors
- **128 Automated Tests** --- Comprehensive test coverage across unit, API, security, and regression categories
- **Data Isolation** --- Branch-level data segregation ensures privacy across locations
