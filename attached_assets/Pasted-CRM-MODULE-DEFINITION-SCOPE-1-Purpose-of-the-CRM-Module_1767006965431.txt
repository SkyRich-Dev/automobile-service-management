CRM MODULE — DEFINITION & SCOPE
1. Purpose of the CRM Module
The CRM (Customer Relationship Management) Module is the central intelligence layer that manages customer relationships across the entire lifecycle:
Lead → Customer → Service → Contract → Retention → Advocacy
It ensures:
Single source of truth for customer data
Personalized service experience
Higher retention & repeat business
Structured follow-ups & accountability
Full traceability of every customer interaction
2. CRM Coverage Model
The CRM must operate across three dimensions:
Customer Dimension – Who the customer is
Interaction Dimension – Every touchpoint
Lifecycle Dimension – Where the customer is in the journey
