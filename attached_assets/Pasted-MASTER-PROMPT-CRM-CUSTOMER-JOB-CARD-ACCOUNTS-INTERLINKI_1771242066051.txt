MASTER PROMPT — CRM ↔ CUSTOMER ↔ JOB CARD ↔ ACCOUNTS INTERLINKING
(End-to-End Data Connection | Zero Duplication | Full Traceability)
🎯 OBJECTIVE
You are a senior enterprise ERP engineering team.
Your task is to design and implement a fully interconnected architecture between:
CRM Module
Customer Master
Job Card / Service Operations
Accounts & Finance
The goal is to ensure:
One single source of truth for customer data
Seamless contract detection
Job card financial traceability
Invoice visibility inside CRM
Real-time status updates
Audit-ready linking
⚠️ No data should be duplicated across modules.
⚠️ All connections must be foreign-key driven and service-layer enforced.
1️⃣ CORE ARCHITECTURE PRINCIPLES
Customer is the root entity
Vehicle is child of customer
Job Card belongs to vehicle
Invoice belongs to job card
Payments belong to invoice
Contracts belong to customer/vehicle
All relationships must be enforced at DB level
2️⃣ CUSTOMER MASTER — PRIMARY ENTITY
Customer entity must connect to:
Vehicles (1-to-many)
Contracts (1-to-many)
Job Cards (via vehicle)
Invoices (via job card)
Payments (via invoice)
CRM interactions
Outstanding balance summary
Customer record must auto-calculate:
Total vehicles
Total services
Total billed
Total paid
Outstanding
Active contracts
3️⃣ CRM ↔ CUSTOMER INTEGRATION
CRM must:
Use Customer ID as primary reference
Show 360° profile:
Vehicles
Service history
Contracts
Invoices
Outstanding
Communication log
CRM events must be linked to:
Job Card ID (if service-related)
Invoice ID (if payment-related)
Contract ID (if AMC-related)
No CRM record without customer linkage.
4️⃣ JOB CARD ↔ CUSTOMER LINK
When creating Job Card:
System must:
Search and validate customer
Select vehicle
Auto-fetch:
Active contracts
Previous service history
Pending invoices
Payment behavior
Display risk indicators:
Overdue payment
Expired contract
Repeat issue
Job Card must store:
Customer ID
Vehicle ID
Contract ID (if applicable)
Linked invoice ID (once created)
5️⃣ CONTRACT AUTO-DETECTION LOGIC
During Appointment / Job Card creation:
System must:
Check if vehicle has active contract
Validate:
Contract validity dates
Service eligibility
Coverage type
If active:
Auto-tag job as “Contract Covered”
Auto-mark covered labor & parts
Lock pricing
Log contract consumption
No manual contract override without manager approval.
6️⃣ JOB CARD ↔ ACCOUNTS INTEGRATION
When job is completed:
System must:
Generate invoice directly from Job Card
Pull:
Labor charges
Parts used
Contract coverage
GST rates
Calculate:
Tax
Discounts
Total payable
Link Invoice to:
Job Card ID
Customer ID
Contract ID (if applicable)
Invoice cannot exist without Job Card reference.

Must display:
Invoice list
Invoice details
Payment history
Outstanding balance
Credit limit status

Job Card reference
Service date

Payment status
8️⃣ PAYMENT ↔ CUSTOMER CONNECTION

Invoice ID
