MASTER PROMPT — CUSTOMER PROFILE & CONTRACT-AWARE CRM
(360° Customer View | Contract Detection | Service & Billing Integration)
ROLE & OBJECTIVE
You are a senior product engineering team (CRM Architect, Service SME, Backend Lead, Frontend Lead) tasked with designing and implementing an enterprise-level Customer Profile within the CRM module, tightly integrated with:
Service Operations
Job Cards
Contracts (AMC / Warranty / Fleet)
Invoices & Accounts
The goal is to provide a single, authoritative customer view and ensure automatic contract detection and application during appointment creation and job card processing.
⚠️ Do NOT change existing workflows or modules
⚠️ Enhance and integrate only
1. CUSTOMER PROFILE — CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLES
One customer = single source of truth
One customer can own multiple vehicles
Every service, invoice, and contract must be traceable to the customer
Contract eligibility must be auto-detected, not manually checked
Customer profile must support operational, financial, and relationship views

2.1 Customer Master Details
Include and display:
Customer ID (system-generated)
Customer type:
Individual
Corporate
Fleet
Customer name
Primary & secondary contact numbers
Email addresses
Address(es)
Preferred branch
Preferred communication channel (WhatsApp / SMS / Email)
Customer category (Retail / VIP / Fleet)
Customer status (Active / Inactive / Blacklisted)

Active

Remaining entitlements
Utilization summary
7. CONTRACT AUTO-DETECTION (CRITICAL REQUIREMENT)
7.1 During Appointment Creation
When creating an appointment:
System must automatically:
Identify customer
Identify selected vehicle
Check for active contracts linked to:
Customer
Vehicle
Display:
“Active Contract Found” badge
Contract type & validity
Covered service types
Prevent manual override unless authorized
7.2 During Job Card Creation
When creating a job card:
System must:
Re-check contract validity (real-time)

Covered labor
Covered parts
Free service eligibility
Visually tag:
Contract-covered items
Non-covered items
Lock pricing for covered items
