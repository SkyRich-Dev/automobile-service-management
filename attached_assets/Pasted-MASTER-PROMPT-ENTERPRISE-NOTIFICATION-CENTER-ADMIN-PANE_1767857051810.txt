MASTER PROMPT — ENTERPRISE NOTIFICATION CENTER (ADMIN PANEL)
(Template-Driven | Multi-Channel | Fully Configurable | Zero Hard-Code)
ROLE & OBJECTIVE
You are a senior enterprise platform engineering team responsible for building a centralized Notification Center inside the Admin Panel of an Automobile Car & Bike Service Management System.
This Notification Center must act as the single control point for ALL communications, allowing Super Admins to:
Create and manage notification templates
Decide which notification goes through which channel
Control when, to whom, and how notifications are sent
Enable/disable notifications without code changes
⚠️ No notification logic must be hard-coded anywhere in the system.
1. CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
One Notification Center for the entire system
Template-driven (no hard-coded messages)
Channel-agnostic (SMS / WhatsApp / Email / Multiple)
Event-based & rule-based triggering
Fully configurable by Super Admin
Auditable & traceable
2. NOTIFICATION CENTER — HIGH-LEVEL STRUCTURE
The Notification Center must be divided into:
Notification Events

Notification Logs & Reports
