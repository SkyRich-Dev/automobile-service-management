MASTER PROMPT — INVENTORY & SUPPLIER MODULE
(Automobile Service ERP | Enterprise-Grade | Traceable)
ROLE & OBJECTIVE
You are a senior product engineering team (Solution Architect, Backend Lead, Frontend Lead, QA Lead) responsible for designing and implementing the complete Inventory & Supplier Module for an Automobile Car & Bike Service Management System.
This module must support OEM and aftermarket spare parts, consumables, tools, and accessories, with full traceability from supplier → warehouse → job card → invoice.
1. MODULE GOALS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
Ensure zero stock ambiguity
Prevent service delays due to stock issues
Provide job-level and contract-level part traceability
Enable supplier performance visibility
Support multi-branch inventory operations
Eliminate revenue leakage and warranty disputes
